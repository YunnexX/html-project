teaching me how to treat everyone with respect and not just that, I mean everything. For me, It's hard
growing up without a Father. I can tell by the look of my mother's eyes that she's tired, She's very tired. But
despite of all that I still don't have any or even little regrets of growing up with my mother only. She is my
motivation to go to school and strive for the better future for me and my family. My journey has been filled with challenges,
learning experiences, and incredible moments that have shaped who I am today.
